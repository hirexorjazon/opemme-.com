    Poems

    To you my love,
let this message fly like a dove.
Soaring high, day and night.
Being with you is like a flight.
I want to see you resting in my chest
and be with you when you’re doing your best.
I maybe far away,
trust me that my love for you will never sway.
We are million miles away,
but my heart will always be with you to stay.
Through thick and thin I’m here,
so please bear with me my dear.
Please don’t doubt my love for you
because this will always stay true.
I love you to the moon and back,
like its white and black.
Stay safe my babe
because my love will never fade.
May we look at the same sky everyday
because I’m yours even if your away.
Je t'aime bébé alors
n'oubliez pas.
	
    Sunrise it is,
seeing your message makes me at ease.
Wishing for your kiss,
so I could feel your lips.
Babe you’re my sunshine,
like the steak and wine.
Wondering how this day will turn out,
and then you see me pout.
“I dreamt of you” I said.
Wishing you were beside me as I was laying in bed.
I thank God for this day,
because my feelings for you was never at bay.
High like the sun,
every time I talk with you I have so much fun.
Wishing this day would never end
because my love for you never bend.
‘I love you babe’ my heart states.
Imagining our Intramuros date.
Kissing you in my head 
because it was you who my heart was lead.
	
    And then the night came,
the love we have is still the same.
I looked into your eyes,
all I could say was Yes!
Yes because I had this ideal lad,
someone that I thought I could never had.
I smile seeing you look at me,
because of you I was tame.
Looking at the stars wishing
and thanking that I am falling.
Falling for someone like you
because you are one of the few.
I kissed you in my dreams,
as if a reflection of reality it seems.
Praying for you to stay
and never walk away.
My heart aches for you alone.
Hoping and wishing to see you soon.
